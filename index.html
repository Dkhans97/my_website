Who am I?

Hi, I'm Dawood Khan, Currently, I work at KNAPP as a Software Commissioning Engineer where I’m responsible for adapting and configuring software solutions to meet each customer’s specific operational needs. My role spans the entire deployment cycle—from testing software in simulation environments to on-site integration and commissioning. I collaborate closely with various technical specialists to ensure seamless implementation and high-quality system performance. In addition to integrating custom features based on client requirements, I also support functional testing and conduct user training before go-live. Once commissioning is complete, I prepare detailed project documentation and ensure a smooth handover to downstream teams.
This role involves working with a broad range of technologies, including object-oriented programming (C++, C#, Java), Python and Bash scripting, relational databases such as SQL and Oracle, and Linux-based environments. It also requires adaptability and strong communication skills, as I frequently travel internationally to deliver tailored solutions and support clients on-site.
